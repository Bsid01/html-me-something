Trip to Florida

My family and I took a roadtrip down to Destin, Fl. On the way there we passed through a few different states and saw different things. Here is some information about the places we visited.

  • Tennessee
  • Alabama
  • Florida

Tennessee

The first major place we went to on our way to Florida was Nashville, Tennessee. The traffic was horrible, and there was a lot of people traveling on the outskirts of the city. Beside all of the people, the city itself was really nice.

Alabama

Next up, there were two major cities we came to in our long journey. The first city was birmingham, and the second was Montgomery, the capitol. These cities are similar to Nashville which means they're bigger than any other city in the state, and they also have a lot of people.

Miramar Beach, Florida

Finally, after 7 hours of travel down south, we made it to our destination which was Miramar Beach, Florida. I've been to Florida once in the past, however I stayed at a different part in the state. In short, Miramar Beach has quite a bit to offer. If one doesn't eat sea food very often, there are some great places like whalestail that that they might enjoy. As for entertainment there's a boardwalk carnival, dolphin cruises, the ocean to swim in, and various stores in the area. After visiting Florida for a second time, I would recommend visiting the beautiful state for anyone looking for a nice vacation.
