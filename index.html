Trip to Florida

My family and I took a roadtrip down to Destin, Fl. On the way there we passed through a few different states and saw different things. Here is some information about the places we visited.

Tennessee

The first major place we went to on our way to Florida was Nashville, Tennessee. The traffic was horrible, and there was a lot of people traveling on the outskirts of the city. Beside all of the people, the city itself was really nice.
